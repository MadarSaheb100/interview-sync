Simultaneous disruption queue rule
If two disruptions are reported within the same minute, the agent queues them and resolves sequentially. Priority: (1) Interviewer cancellation, (2) Room conflicts, (3) Candidate delays. After resolving disruption 1, agent re-checks all constraints before applying disruption 2 — the first swap may have freed or blocked resources affecting the second resolution.
