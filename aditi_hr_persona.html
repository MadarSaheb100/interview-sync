Background

Manages end-to-end hiring for Engineering & Product at a 200-person startup scaling to 350
Runs 4–6 Super Days per quarter; no dedicated scheduling tool beyond Outlook and a shared Google Sheet
Collaborates with 3 hiring managers and relies on interviewer goodwill for last-minute coverage
Often operates with 1-hour prep time between panels and no dedicated coordinator support

Goals

Deliver a seamless, professional candidate experience — even when chaos hits behind the scenes
Maintain panel seniority balance if someone drops — not just fill a warm body in the slot
Communicate proactively to candidates and interviewers without sounding disorganised
Reduce post-Super Day debrief gaps caused by missed or swapped interview sequences

Pain points

No real-time view of interviewer availability across rooms — relies on manual calls and Slack messages
Cascade effect: a single VP cancellation breaks room allocation, candidate sequence, and debrief order simultaneously
Candidates receive conflicting or late updates, damaging employer brand for high-priority hires
No substitute-interviewer registry — she has to mentally recall who is qualified and available on the spot
Juggling WhatsApp, email, calendar invites, and a Google Sheet simultaneously under time pressure
Recovery decisions must still honour seniority, interview type (technical/behavioural), and conflict-of-interest rules

The critical moment — 1:45 PM

Senior VP (scheduled 2:00 PM, Room B) messages: "Something came up, can't make it"
Candidate for that slot is already in the waiting area; two more slots depend on this room being freed on time
Aditi must identify a qualified replacement, get confirmation, update the room booking, re-notify the candidate, and alert the debrief coordinator — all within 10 minutes
If she fails, the candidate either waits visibly or the interview is cancelled — both outcomes hurt offer acceptance rates

Tools currently used

Outlook Calendar for invite management
Google Sheets as the "source of truth" schedule
WhatsApp groups for real-time coordination
Lever (ATS) for candidate records — not used live on the day

What she needs from a solution

Instant substitute suggestions ranked by availability, seniority, and conflict rules
Auto-cascade: room re-assignment, candidate re-notification, and debrief update in one action
Audit trail she can share with hiring managers post-event
Works within tools she already uses — no new login under pressure
